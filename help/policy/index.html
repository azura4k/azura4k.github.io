As applicable during stated time frame, the Privacy Policy shall be an agreement between the Company

not sold by a central party or person. We affirm that any user info collected and stored shall not be directly
detrimental to an end user’s health, finances, or legal status. This Agreement shall apply to all company
content, services, websites, and all current or future subsidiaries as per the writing and publishing
of this agreement

Section 1: Non-Prompted collected data


Our software and services shall reserve the right to collect the following Client data and store it for use:
1. The Operating System Details of which the End User is operating the Contractor’s Software on.
2. Website Cookies and other standard web data used by the Browser.
3. Third Party Tracking Technologies and Information.
4. Server and Data Logs of the Companies’ private servers or technologies.
5. Diagnostics Information that is non-identifiable to the Client.

Section 2: Prompted Collected Data


With the express permission of the Client, the Company shall achieve and hold the right to collect any
information that is given by the client to complete a certain or specified task. Once said task or service is
completed, any information that is disclosed between the Client and Company shall be either erased, or
in the case of contact information and payment logs. Any confidential information, such as a social
security number or credit/debt card information, shall be erased from company databases or data
storing hardware effective immediately after the completion of a task or service with the given
exception of the Client prompting to store said information. The Client shall agree that any information
or data stored by a third-party service is not controllable by the Company shall be the responsibility of
the Client to arbitrate with the third party for data management

Section 3: Data Usage

The Company shall reserve the right to use all Data stored for the following purposes:
1. Legal Records
2. Financial Records
3. Data Analytics
4. Software Diagnostics
5. Software Performance Reporting
6. Proof of Purchase or Proof of Service
7. Identity Verification or Authentication
8. Licensing Software to Client
9. Transaction Processing (via POS Services of a Third Party

Section 4: security of data

We at this Company believe in the security of our Client’s Data. That’s why no exclusively identifying
information is stored when collected. All software and licensing information is stored using other Server
and Database Companies, in of which none of the Client’s personal data is stored. Client Contact
Information and Addresses are stored in a secured third party service or company computers and servers.. In of which the
information is locked and protected from non-authorized affiliates and intruders. As to protect our
Clients personal information.
