THe main aspect of a business

With limited liability, your personal assets and liability is protected from any legal prosecution against your business. This liability will apply and treat your business as a separate entity, and not as a branch of your person.

F  A  Q

What is an EIN?

An EIN is an Identification Number used by the US Federal Government and other financial institutions Your EIN can be equated to your Social Security Number, so do not give it away to un-trusted sources.

Why would I want an LLC?

An LLC is used for capital ventures one may consider too risky for personal liability. LLCs act as their own legal entity to a certain extent of the law in each State. You would want to have one for risky business ventures.

Which Option is Better?

Depending on your needs and what you want to gain from this. Certain professions will require permits for different locations, while other may want to legally copyright or patent their product. Look at the type of field and decide which is best for you.
