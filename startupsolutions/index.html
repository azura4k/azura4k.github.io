Why Register A Business?

Limited Liability

To protect your personal assets and net worth from lawsuits or other legal affairs. You would wish to have an LLC or other Registered Busienss for the benifits it can give to you, and the capital ventures you can take without fear of extreme backlash.
(Note I am not a legal attorney and this is not legal advice)

Professional Access

Certain Services and tools are only accesable to registered businesses, such as business checking accounts and business credit accounts. These services can smooth over the conduct of business and establish a good relationship with other businesses in the community.

Open Opportunity

What may not be done on a personal level, can come to fruition on a commercial level. Access to grants, loans, funds, and other capital veins can give you the boost you need to vitalize your endevours.
