Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EIN

An EIN is an Identification Number used by the US Federal Government and other Financial Institutions to verify a Business's Legitimacy and for Taxation purposes with Employees. Your business would need an EIN to open a business bank account, or file your commercial taxes and get business loans. Your EIN can be equated to your Social Security Number, so dont give it away to non-trusted sources.

Why would I want an LLC?

An LLC is used for capital ventures one may consider too risky for personal liability. LLCs act as their own legal entity to a certain extent of the law in each State. Although their main premise is to do business while being personally protected from legal lawsuits and affairs to a certain degree. So if your taking a business venture such as a car lot or plumbing service, you will not have to worry about personally defaulting on a loan, or being sued because your employee made an error.
